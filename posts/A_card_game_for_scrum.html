A card game for scrum

Feb 25, 2013

Sometimes life as a Scrum Master can be difficult. An advice can be easily interpreted to an order or even an uncontructive word. So I invented a card game that can help team members to sit and enjoy a good time together with a fun card game which they can learn more about Scrum without any real life pain.

Rules:

Single Sprint game.

  1. Player who can burn all Story Cards at the end of the sprint is the winner.
  2. One sprint should be 7 or 14 turns. So each turn will represent a day in a sprint.
  3. If two or more players can burn all cards at the end of the sprint then one who can burn more Business Point is the winner.
  4. If players can not burn all cards then a player who can burn the most Business Point is the winner.
  5. All players have to draw at least 5 Story Cards randomly at their first turn (Sprint Planning Turn). Number of cards can be added depends on player's decision.
  6. Each Story Card has a predefined set of Business Point and Story Point.
  7. Player will earn 5 Burn down Point for every single turn except for the first turn. They can use Burn down Point to burn Story Cards or use it to buy an Action Card.
  8. Player can use their Burn down Point to draw an Action Card at the start of each turn. Action Card can prevent a bad effect from a Scenario Card. Buying an Action Card after drawing a Scenario Card is not allowed.
  9. Each turn player have to draw a Scenario Card. This kind of card effect their Story Point and may require player to draw another card.
  10. Each turn players have to select their working Story Card. Some Scenario Card will effect only working Story Card. If no Story Card selected an effect from Scenario Card would be void and they can not burn any Story Point in that turn.
  11. Players have to perform a Mid Sprint Turn at the 4th turn of seven days sprint or the 7th turn of fourteen days sprint. In this turn their Burn down Point will be reduced by 2. Players can return any number of Story Card in this turn. Returned story won't give any Business Point.

Multi Sprints game

  1. Rules for multi sprints are exactly the same with single sprint game except the score calculation will be performed at the end of the last sprint.
  2. Unfinished card have to be added to the next sprint's backlog with all remaining Story Point
  3. Unused Action Card also remain in the next sprint.
  4. For a players who has closed all Story Card in the current sprint and still have some turns left. In this case players has an option to use their point to buy an Action Card each turn to prepare for the next sprint. They also has an option to draw or not draw a Scenario Card each turn as well.

Card's Description

Story Card

A story card represent User's Story in real scrum process. It's consist of predefined Business Point and Story Point. Players can not choose their story cards but they're free to accept any number of cards in each Sprint Planning Turn and they can return any number of cards at the Mid Sprint Turn.

Scenario Card

A scenario card represent a real situation in software development process. Some are good, more are bad. Each card may has good and bad effect to the Burn down Point. Some card may require player to draw another Story Card or Scenario Card. Same like a surprise feature that can either introduce new bugs, require more work or adding more business value at the same time.

Below are all Scenario Cards and effects.

Just a boring day.
No effect.
Job well done!
Add 1 Burn down Point to the current turn.
Double performance!!
Multiply the restBurn down Point by two just for the current turn.
Burn them all!!!
Clear current Story Card free. Have to use with Action Card: Well Plan.
Under estimate.
Add 2 Story Point to a working Story Card.
Over estimate.
Reduce 2 Story Point from a working Story Card.
Unexpected Meeting.
Reduce 2 Burn down Point.
Sick.
Reduce 3 Burn down Point.
Git issue.
Reduce 3 Burn down Point.
Wait.
Working Story Card is freezed for 2 turn. Players can select a new working Story Card and start burn new card at the same turn.
Bug.
Add 1 Story Point to any Story Card, Draw a Scenario Card.
Surprise Story.
Draw a Story Card. Reduce Business Point of the new card by 50%.
Missile!
Draw a Story Card, Reduce Burn down Point by 2.
Dependency
Swap working Story Card with another card. If no card to swap then this story will be freezed until the end of the sprint.
Initiative.
Reduce 1 Burn down Point, Draw a Story Card, Draw a Scenario Card.
Stand up pay back.
Clear all freezed Story Card.

Action Card

Action card represent an effort of a team member who try to make his/her every day job smoother and easier. Action card can prevent or reduce a bad effect of a Scenario Card. Some can also boots your Burn down Point which will last long to the end of the game.

Below are all Action Card and its effects.

Well Collaborative.
Clear all freezed Story Cards at once.
Do it tomorrow.
Push a Story Card to the next sprint. For the last sprint or a single sprint game simply return a Story Card to the deck.
Pair Programming.
Recover 2 Burn down Point from Scenario Card: Unexpected Meeting, Sick.
Good Convention.
Recover 1 Burn down Point from a Scenario Card: Sick, Unexpected meeting or Eliminate a Scenario Card: Git issue or just use it to burn 1 Story Point.
Best Practice.
Add 1 Burn down Point every turn start from the next turn until the end of the game.
Well plan
Clear 1 Story Card free. Have to use with Scenario Card: Burn them all!!!
