A card game for scrum

Feb 25, 2013

Sometimes life as a Scrum Master can be difficult. An advice can be easily interpreted to an order or even an uncontructive word. So I invented a card game that can help team members to sit and enjoy a good time together with a fun card game which they can learn more about Scrum without the real life pain.

Rules:

  1. Player who can burn all Story Cards at the end of the sprint is the winner.
  2. One sprint should be 7 or 14 turns. So each turn will represent a day in a sprint.
  3. If two or more players can burn all cards at the end of the sprint then one who can burn more Business Point is the winner.
  4. If players can not burn all cards then a player who can burn the most Business Point is the winner.
  5. All players have to draw at least 5 Story Cards randomly at their first turn (Sprint Planning Turn). Number of cards can be added depends on player's decision.
  6. Each Story Card has a predefined set of Business Point and Story Point.
  7. Player will earn 5 Burn down Point for every single turn except the first turn. They can use Burn down Point to burn Story Cards or use it to buy an Action Card.
  8. Player can use their Burn down Point to draw an Action Card at the start of each turn. Buying an Action Card after drawing a Scenario Card is not allowed.
  9. Each turn player have to draw a Scenario Card. This kind of card effect their Story Point and Business Point.
  10. Player can use an Action Card to prevent a bad effect from a Scenario Card.
